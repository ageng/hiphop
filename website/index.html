HipHop is an application for Windows, Mac and Linux that lets you listen instantly to more than 26 million songs. It requires no sign up, displays no ads and is 100% safe.

We believe that listening to music should always be free and available to everyone, and that only owning it should bare a cost.

We also believe that a software both useful and open-source simply can't die.
